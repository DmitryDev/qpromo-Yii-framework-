Preload & Data Services

We offer a variety innovative options to deliver your custom content, whether it is a simple PDF catalog, a web page set to autorun, or a dazzling Flash presentation that loads automatically. Our preload options can add power and lasting impact to your promotional marketing campaign. For more advanced content delivery like software distribution and other kinds of integrated data solutions, we offer RTS portable security software, portable freeware applications, custom icon creation, and much more. See below for details and call or send an email to sales@qpromo.com to discuss your project.
Important Note: With the exception of the Standard Preload, the above options will only work reliably on a Windows-based computer (and newer than Windows 98). Talk to a sales rep for info on how to have content accesible on a Mac while still having non-standard options work on a Windows PC.

  • Standard Preload

    We copy your content to your flash drives and users can access it at will. Perfect for a "soft sell" or for distributing software and other data content to users who know what they're getting.

  • Menu-Prompt Autorun

    When users insert their flash drives, Window's default menu-prompt will feature a custom option, pointing the users to access your content. Great for getting your info across without appearing aggressive.

  • AutoPlay Autorun

    Our most direct option. As soon as the USB Flash Drive is plugged in it will send information to Windows in order to automatically launch your content. *The AutoPlay feature can be disabled from Windows by the user or an administrator. The content will remain accesible on the Flash Drive but it will not launch automatically in this case.

  • RTS Portable Security Suite

    Partnered with Research Triangle Software Inc., Qpromo now offers a great suite of USB applications for security and privacy needs. Click here for more detailed information on these powerful new applications. Find out more

  • Portable Apps

    Portable apps--software programs that you can run on a PC without installing them on the host computer--are becoming very popular and are now widely available. Many favorite freeware apps like the Mozilla Firefox browswer and Pidgin messenger client now come in portable versions that you can run directly off a Flash Drive. We're happy to preload your entire order with any such eligible apps. Click here for more on portable apps.

  • Non-Erasable Content

    Make all or some of your preload content files non-erasable by locking them in a CD-ROM partition; your PC will treat this section of your Flash Drive storage space as if it were a CD that cannot be deleted or overwritten.

  • Hidden Files

    Choose to have certain files hidden from normal user access, such as support-files that may clutter an otherwise well-organized Flash Drive or data-collecting files that cannot be locked nor compromised.

  • Serialization Service

    Select a range of hexadecimal values to be serialized in each unit's device properties.

  • Custom Icon Creation

    A customized icon to replace the default drive icon associated with your Flash Drive device. A very popular option for Portable Media Player orders, value-added resellers, and software/media distributors.

  • USB Content Updating And Drive Tracking

    USB TurnKey is embedded software that allows you to securely update your pre-load files. Track ROI through comprehensive usage statistics. Each drive is paired with an individual user so you know who and when accesses your content. USB Drives can be used as security keys to access protected data on line or over a network.
